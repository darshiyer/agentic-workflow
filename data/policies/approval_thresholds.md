# Approval Thresholds Policy

## Routing rules
- **Under $1,000**: Auto-approve if the vendor is low-risk and no policy exceptions exist.
- **$1,000 to $10,000**: Require line-manager approval. Route to the department manager listed on the PO.
- **$10,000 to $50,000**: Require CFO or finance director approval.
- **Above $50,000**: Require board-level approval and must be included in the cash-flow forecast.

## Exceptions
- Any invoice from a high-risk vendor must be escalated one level above the amount-based rule.
- Invoices without a valid PO number require AP supervisor review regardless of amount.
- Duplicate invoice numbers for the same vendor must be held pending investigation.

## Delegation
- Delegated approvers are allowed only if recorded in the ERP delegation registry.
